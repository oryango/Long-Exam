Admissions Office

Malayan Colleges Mindanao is now accepting applicants for Senior High School and College Applications for SY: 2021-2022 1st Semester!

Start your journey to academic excellence now!

To apply, please follow these simple steps:

  1. MCM Admissions Examination is waived for SY 2021-2022. Applicants for Senior High School and College may register online through this link: bit.ly/ApplicantRegistrationWebsite

  2. Pay the applicant registration fee of Php 300. You may pay at the MCM Treasury Office anytime from Mondays to Fridays, from 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM, except duing declared holidays.

  3. To validate your application and payment, email a clear photo of your current school ID (or any proof of enrollment) to admissions@mcm.edu.ph and send a screenshot of your payment transaction confirmation at treasury@mcm.edu.ph.

Registrar's Office

The Office of the Registrar provides a wide range of services to the academic community. The office aims to provide services in an efficient manner that allows the members of the academic community to use the services with minimum difficulty and maximum satisfaction. Our standing policy is always to respond to legitimate requests for information or services as quickly and accurately as possible.

The Office of the Registrar is responsible for the collection, administration, maintenance, processing, and distribution of information. As official custodian of all student academic records, the registrar determines the access and distribution of those records based on school policy.
